01 — About

What I do

Most "AI in QA" work today means wiring an LLM into an existing framework as a helper. I went a level deeper: the platform I architected treats natural language as the primary interface, not an add-on — a QE task described in plain English is the input, and validated requirements, generated test cases, and executed Playwright scripts are the output. That reframing is what let one platform scale to 9 product teams without a per-team fork.

I'm Wipro's Best Delivery Champion 2025 for AI/ML testing transformation at a Fortune 500 hospitality & gaming enterprise, where I serve as Principal QE Architect leading AI-Driven Lifecycle (AI-DLC) initiatives. The platform cuts test-authoring-to-execution time 30–40% against the prior manual Playwright workflow — I lead and coach a core group of 15–20 QE engineers directly across 15 Agile teams, with the remaining teams self-serving off the shared framework and reusable prompt templates spanning Commerce, Identity, Payment, and 15 more domains.
